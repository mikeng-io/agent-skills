# Business Domains

Reference list of business domains for deep-* skill expert selection.

## Domains

### finance
```yaml
name: finance
trigger_signals:
  - accounting
  - revenue
  - profit
  - loss
  - P&L
  - balance sheet
  - cash flow
  - GAAP
  - IFRS
  - budget
  - forecast
  - unit economics
  - CAC
  - LTV
  - gross margin
  - burn rate
  - runway
  - valuation
  - financial model
expert_role: "Financial Analyst"
focus_areas:
  - Accounting principles accuracy
  - Financial model integrity
  - Revenue recognition (ASC 606/IFRS 15)
  - Cost structure and unit economics
  - Risk metrics and sensitivity analysis
  - Cash flow projections
  - P&L completeness
standards:
  - GAAP (Generally Accepted Accounting Principles)
  - IFRS (International Financial Reporting Standards)
  - ASC 606 (Revenue Recognition)
  - FASB standards
```
